About the Project

Why a lost-and-found portal is useful on campus

Many students lose items such as ID cards, phones, books, chargers, and keys around campus. A lost-and-found portal gives the school one clear place to post missing and recovered items, making it easier for people to reconnect with what they lost.

Project Goals

  • Describe a real campus problem.
  • Show how a static web portal could help.
  • Include the required project details and navigation.
  • Prepare the site for GitHub Pages deployment.

How the portal would work

The portal would let users browse found items, report lost items, and contact the person who posted a match. Because it is static, it focuses on presenting the service clearly rather than running a full database-backed system.
